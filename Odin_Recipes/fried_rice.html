Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Soya sauce
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Boil the rice
  3. Fry a couple of eggs in a pan
  4. Add the coocked rice and soya sauce to the pan, mix
  5. Add the chicken and vegetables
